# Road-Accident-Data-Analysis(Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)
## Project Overview

This project analyzes road accident data in the United Kingdom to uncover patterns, risk factors, and trends related to road casualties. Using Microsoft Excel, I designed an interactive dashboard that transforms raw accident data into actionable insights through carefully defined KPIs and visual analysis.

The primary objective of this project is to analyze road accidents occurring in the UK, with a strong focus on understanding casualty severity, accident conditions, and contributing factors such as vehicle type, road characteristics, time of day, and location.

This dashboard is intended to support data-driven decision-making for road safety analysis, policy evaluation, and public awareness.

## Objectives

Analyze total casualties resulting from road accidents in the UK

Identify how accident severity impacts casualty distribution

Compare casualty trends across years and months

Examine the influence of vehicle type, road type, road surface, and location

Understand how time (day, night, time of day) and area (urban vs rural) relate to accident outcomes
# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
### Primary KPIs

Total Casualties after Accidents

Total Casualties and Percentage Distribution by Accident Severity

Maximum Casualties Recorded

### Secondary KPIs

Total Casualties by Vehicle Type

Monthly Trend Analysis (Current Year vs Previous Year)

Maximum Casualties by Road Type

Distribution of Casualties by Road Surface

Casualty Analysis by Area, Location, and Time

Urban vs Rural

Day vs Night

Time of Day
